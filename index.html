Northwest Arizona Real Estate

Dirt Road Real Estate is a full service residential and rural property specialist agency working in Northwestern Arizona, including the Kingman, Seligman, Wikieup, and Bullhead City areas, among others. Our team is happy to help both buyers and sellers find the perfect deal for their property or home.

Services Tailored To Our Area

Our services include property showings, brokerage services, and knowledge of the area in a way that many more distantly located agents can't match. We can interface with inspectors, work with loan officers, and work with liability and insurance personnel. We have no requirements for a property that can be sold; we are willing to sell unimproved land. Some of our specialties include haul water properties, properties on septic, and properties on well water.

City Limits Are No Obstacle

While Dirt Road does provide specialty services and advice relating to rural and unimproved properties, we are also qualified and experienced with real estate within town limits as well. If you are looking to buy or sell real estate in Arizona, get in touch with us today.
