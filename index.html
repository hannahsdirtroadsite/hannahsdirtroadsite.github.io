Northwest Arizona Real Estate

Dirt Road Real Estate is a full service residential and rural property brokerage working in Northwestern Arizona, including the Kingman, Seligman, Wikieup, Golden Valley, Bullhead City and all areas in between. Our team is happy to help buyers and sellers find the property or home.

Specific Expertise Has Unmatchable Value

Dirt Road Agents live and work in the areas they service, they possess intimate knowledge of the neighborhoods, ranches, customs and local restrictions. Our Agents have acquired lending on very challenging and unique. If you have been told it cannot be done, you need to reach out to a Dirt Road Agent. We can interface with inspectors, with loan officers, and liability/insurance personnel. We have excellent contractors as referrals for all your development needs.

We LOVE handling vacant, unimproved land, we do it on purpose! We handle: water haul, septic, and wells on a day to day basis and are experts on the specific requirements of each. Some of our specialties include the ability to read maps, determine legal access, and actually verify you are looking at the correct property. (Yes this is truly a real concern!)

City Limits Are No Obstacle

Dirt Road Realtors are qualified and experienced with residential real estate within city limits as well. If you are looking to buy or sell real estate in Northwest Arizona, get in touch with us today.
