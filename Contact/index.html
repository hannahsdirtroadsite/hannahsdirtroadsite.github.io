We Are Happy To Assist You!

At Dirt Road Real Estate we do not operate “teams” as you have had referenced in other real estate firms, we are a team, all of us. If one of us suggests that another team member should be helping you, that means that person feels that they are the expert in that area and they want you to get the very best expert assistance that DRRE can give you.

Because of our expertise we stay very busy so often times to get into our schedule you may need to schedule weeks ahead. We are typically not able to take “oh by the way” today service because much of what we may want to see/show needs to be mapped, scheduled and planned with the sellers. Please the moment you have even a tentative schedule reach out and tentatively schedule with us; we'd rather have the time saved 'just in case' and have to cancel/postpone than have you show up and not have the time or preparation to properly serve your needs. This applies especially to customers who need to travel!



Our Coverage Area

To the right is a road map with Dirt Road Real Estate's range highlighted. We are happy to sell properties from Lake Mead down to Kingman, and across to Ash Fork and Wikieup. Click on one of the thumbnails below to see different maps with our range highlighted.

The geography of Northwest Arizona begins at around sea level at the Colorado River in the west, and then starts sloping gently upward the further east you go. Kingman, AZ, is 3,300 feet high. Ash Fork is around 5,000 feet high. The desert floor in the west offers temperate winters with slightly warmer summers, while the mountains in the east offer temperate summers and light, occasional snow in the winter. There's something for everyone!
